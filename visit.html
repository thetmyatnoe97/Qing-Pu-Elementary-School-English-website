Transport options

Qing Pu is one of the best-connected schools in Taoyuan — here is every way to reach us.

8 mins

THSR Taoyuan Station

高鐵桃園站

  1. Take the Taiwan High Speed Rail to Taoyuan Station
  2. Exit the station and board a free shuttle bus or taxi
  3. Arrive at the school gate in approximately 8 minutes walk

The THSR connects Taoyuan to Taipei Main Station in 20 minutes and to Zuoying (Kaohsiung) in 75 minutes.

11 mins

Airport MRT — Station A18

機場捷運 A18 高鐵桃園站

  1. Board the Taoyuan Airport MRT (purple line)
  2. Alight at A18 — HSR Taoyuan Station
  3. Walk approximately 11 minutes northeast to the school

Direct connection to Taoyuan International Airport (A13) — ideal for international visitors arriving by air.

At the school gate

Parking available

By car

開車前往

  • On-campus parking is very limited and primarily reserved for staff. Visitor parking is only available by prior arrangement with the school office or during approved school events. Visitors are advised to use nearby public parking facilities in the Qingpu / Taoyuan HSR area when available.

Neighbourhood

What's nearby

Qing Pu sits at the heart of Taoyuan's newest district — surrounded by cultural and transport landmarks.

Taoyuan Museum of Fine Arts

5 mins walk

A striking contemporary art museum opened in 2021. Grade 4 students visit each semester as part of their integrated activities unit.

Map →

Taoyuan International Baseball Stadium

10 mins walk

Home to the Rakuten Monkeys (CPL). The stadium is visible from the school rooftop and hosts international matches throughout the year.

Map →

Hengshan Calligraphy Art Center and Park

8 mins walk

An outdoor cultural park celebrating Chinese calligraphy and art. Used by the school for outdoor drawing and cultural heritage activities.

Map →

Taoyuan International Airport

20 mins (Airport MRT)

Taiwan's main international gateway — 20 minutes via Airport MRT from A18. Students see departing aircraft from the playground on clear days.

Map →

Gloria Outlet

10 journal-mini-card__desc

One of Taiwan's largest outlet malls, with international brands, dining, and entertainment — a popular weekend destination for families in the area.

Map →

IKEA Taoyuan

10 mins

A full-size IKEA store in the Qingpu district — one of the largest in northern Taiwan. Convenient for families in the area.

Map →

Xpark Aquarium

15 mins

A premium aquarium with marine life exhibits, interactive zones, and educational programmes — a favourite field trip and family outing destination.

Map →

Parks & green spaces

青埔公園 & surrounding area

The Qingpu district is home to several parks and green open spaces — a peaceful counterpoint to the busy transport hubs, and a regular venue for outdoor school activities.
