Bilingual teachers


Our dedicated bilingual teaching team consists of foreign teachers, English teachers, and homeroom teachers who work together to create a dynamic and supportive learning environment. Through collaborative lesson planning and innovative teaching strategies, our educators help students develop strong communication skills, global awareness, and confidence in using English.



By integrating language learning with subject content and international exchange activities, our teachers inspire students to become active learners, critical thinkers, and responsible global citizens.


Strong local and international teaching team — dynamic in style, dedicated in purpose. Meet the 23 educators who make bilingual learning happen every day at Qing Pu.
