🌐 English education

Bilingual programmes by grade

Following Taiwan's 2030 Bilingual Nation policy, we teach core subjects in both English and Mandarin across all six grades.

Gr. 1–2

Bilingual Life Studies

Children explore the world through bilingual stories, songs, and hands-on activities.

  • International Education
  • Life Studies (bilingual)
  • English conversation
Gr. 3–4

All Together

Integrated activities connect English to real-world community experiences.

  • All Together integrated activities
  • International Education
  • English reading & writing
Gr. 5–6

PE & Art

Specialist subjects taught by bilingual teachers — PE games, art critique, and creative projects, all in English.

  • PE & Art (bilingual)
