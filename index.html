Bilingual Programmes by Grade

Following Taiwan's 2030 Bilingual Nation policy, we teach core subjects in both English and Mandarin across all six grades.

Children explore the world through bilingual stories, songs, and hands-on activities.

  • Life Studies (bilingual)
  • English conversation

All Together
